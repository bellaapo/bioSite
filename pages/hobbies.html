Hobbies

Rayce is passionate about disc golf, nature exploration, and frog identification. These hobbies allow him to stay active, connect with the outdoors, and fuel his curiosity for wildlife. Each activity plays a meaningful role in his life, offering both relaxation and personal growth.

Rayce has a deep passion for disc golf, a sport he began playing in April 2022. His love for the game goes beyond just physical activity; he is captivated by the nature aspect and particularly enjoys observing how the discs fly and how far he can throw them.

Rayce's connection to the outdoors is further reflected in his interest in exploring nature, with canoeing on the Silver River in Ocala with our father being one of his most cherished experiences.

Rayce has a special talent for identifying frogs. His interest in these creatures started in childhood and has grown into a passion for understanding their various species and habitats.
